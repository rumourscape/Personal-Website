Blog 1

I have always been wary of the threat possessed by the internet towards user privacy. And I tend to think thrice before posting anything online.
That being said, it begs the question: Why this, Why now?

Glad you asked.
Well, you see; I write. And writing that isn't published might as well have not been written at all. So, I needed a home for my blogs and a self-built one at that. Thus, this.
This isn't my first foray into Web-development. I have been writing html code ever since my 7th grade. I owned the website on the domain 'advait.ga' from 2018-19 which I lost after I forgot to re-register it (stupid me). So, what I lacked was commitment.

So here's to a new start. And hopefully, this might get me to write some more, and relatively regularly too.
Happy Reading!

"Either write something worth reading or do something worth writing."
